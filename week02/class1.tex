Euclid's algorithm
\begin{itemize}
\item Step 1 If m < n, exchange m and n.
\item Step 2 Divide m by n and get the reamiander, r. If r=0, report n as GCD of m and n.
\item Step 3 Replace m by n and replace n by r. Repeat Step 2.
\end{itemize}
